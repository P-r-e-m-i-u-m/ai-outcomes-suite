# PRD: AI Tutor That Guarantees Outcomes

## Niche

SAT Math outcome tutor.

## Problem

Students and parents pay for generic learning apps but cannot see exact skill gaps, readiness, or a credible improvement plan.

## MVP

- Track SAT Math attempts by skill.
- Calculate mastery from accuracy, difficulty, and speed.
- Identify weakest skills.
- Generate a seven-day practice plan.
- Show a clear outcome policy.

## Target Users

- SAT students.
- Parents paying for tutoring.
- Small tutoring centers.

## Differentiation

Most apps show content. This product shows a measurable path to a score outcome.

## Success Metrics

- Weekly active learners.
- Questions completed per learner.
- Mock test score improvement.
- Percentage of students completing the plan.
- Parent conversion after diagnostic.

## First Paid Version

- Diagnostic test.
- Parent dashboard.
- Tutor review mode.
- Question bank import.
- Weekly score forecast.

## Risks

- "Guarantee" language needs careful legal and marketing review.
- Question quality must be high.
- Students may churn if plans feel repetitive.
