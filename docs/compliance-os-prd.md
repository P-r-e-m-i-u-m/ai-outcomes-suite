# PRD: AI Compliance OS

## Problem

AI SaaS teams need logs, audit trails, user notices, model documentation, human oversight, and safety review evidence. They do not want to build this paperwork from scratch.

## MVP

- SDK-style logger.
- Prompt and response records.
- Model, purpose, consent, safety checks, risk level, and data categories.
- Human oversight queue.
- Compliance report and model card generation.

## Target Users

- SaaS startups adding AI features.
- Agencies building AI workflows for clients.
- Internal tooling teams.

## Differentiation

The wedge is developer experience: one SDK call creates audit-ready operational evidence.

## Success Metrics

- SDK installs.
- Weekly logged AI events.
- Reports generated.
- Number of teams with human review workflows.

## First Paid Version

- Hosted dashboard.
- Data retention controls.
- Policy templates.
- Export to PDF/CSV.
- Webhooks for high-risk events.
- Role-based access controls.

## Risks

- Compliance requirements vary by jurisdiction.
- Product must avoid claiming legal certification.
- Logs may contain sensitive data and need strong security controls.
