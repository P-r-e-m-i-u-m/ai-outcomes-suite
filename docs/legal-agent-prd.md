# PRD: Personal Legal Agent

## Problem

Consumers and small businesses lose money because legal documents, refund denials, job contracts, rental agreements, and insurance letters are hard to understand.

## MVP

- Paste or upload document text.
- Detect document type.
- Summarize in plain English.
- Flag risks and deadlines.
- Draft a polite action letter.

## Target Users

- Renters.
- Employees reviewing job terms.
- Consumers dealing with denied claims or refunds.
- Small business owners.

## Differentiation

The product is first-line legal defense: fast, cheap, and plain-language. It does not replace lawyers.

## Success Metrics

- Documents analyzed.
- Letters generated.
- User-reported money recovered or issues resolved.
- Conversion to paid review.

## First Paid Version

- PDF/DOCX upload.
- Jurisdiction-aware rules.
- Deadline reminders.
- Letter sending and tracking.
- Lawyer marketplace referral.

## Risks

- Must avoid unauthorized practice of law.
- Needs strong disclaimers and careful jurisdiction handling.
- Sensitive user data requires strong privacy controls.
